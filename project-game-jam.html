Game Jam

Visual identity + social media

The ‘Indies mx Game Jam’ was a game development event organized by ‘Indies mx’ in collaboration with other studios. The purpose was to create a traditional game jam in which teams are given a timeframe to design and make a video game. The winners were granted an opportunity to participate in the final round held in Brazil.

For this event, I was put in charge of the visual identity, including designing stream elements. My first task was to integrate the Indies logo into the overall brand of the event. I decided to integrate this into a school styled emblem; this to represent the learning opportunities that game jams bring.

For design elements, I used common elements in gaming, such as game controllers and geometrical shapes like squares and X’s. I also used patterns like grids and diagonal lines, as well as bright and fun colors.
