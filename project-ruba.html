Ruba App

UI design + mobile app

Ruba is a national real estate development company here in Mexico. They specialize in the construction of homes in self-owned gated communities, where they also offer spaces such as parks, gyms and pools.

The app consisted of the following features:

  • * House catalog allowing users to browse existing homes on the market.
  • * Community section where users can get in touch with other existing members of their gated community by sharing articles and chatting.
  • * Rewards program gifting prizes to users who recommend their family members and friends for new homes that result in sales.
  • * Claims system where home owners can report incidents in their homes.
