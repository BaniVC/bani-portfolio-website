about me

Ever since middle school, I've always been more in touch with my artistic side, doodling in all my notebooks and making random sketches. I later started making my own pieces of art with geometrical shapes and shading techniques. This continued in high school and soon I started spending all my time on my computer making wallpapers and editing music videos. These hobbies were fundamental when I decided to study graphic design.

I'm really lucky I turned a hobby into a career, and can confidently say I chose the right path. I've focused my work on digital design, especially on UI design and motion graphics; more recently I have dived into the world of front-end development. Learning the skills necessary to transform my designs into code is the next step into my career.

I am currently working as a freelance web designer, improving my abilities with each new project.
