Binmatter

Visual identity + logo redesign + web design + social media

Binmatter is a company that focuses on user-friendly applications and the development of video games. While I was working for the company, I was assigned to freshen up the brand and give it a unified image.

I started with the redesign of the logo, simplifying it and updating the typography. I tried to showcase a more 'tech' vibe with new bright colors and elements repsesenting circuits such as dots, lines,squares and computers.

I followed up with the website design, following the circuit theme. and set the overall mood of the company's social media.
