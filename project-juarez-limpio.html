Mi Juarez Limpio

Mobile app + UI Design + visual identity + logo design

Client

This was an internal project for the company I was working for at the time. It was part of an initiative to improve our city using technology. All local businesses were encouraged to participate.

My role

I was the only designer on this project.

Project overview

Mi Juárez Limpio is a mobile app targeted at local residents in Ciudad Juárez. It’s essentially a social media app focused on posting pictures. The objective was to create an app where the public could post positive photos, showcasing the beauty of our city, but also make claims about damaged infrastructure and streets. By exposing problems, we wanted to put pressure on the local government to implement solutions.

We also wanted to include a section where people could post blog posts where people can post advice and other entries.

Problem

Ciudad Juárez is a border city in the state of Chihuahua, Mexico. It is unfortunately infamous for violence, insecurity and poor city planning. This has caused many inconveniences throughout the city’s infrastructure like: potholes, piles of trash, traffic accidents, graffiti, etc.

Goals

  • Improve the circumstances of our streets; fixing potholes, general maintenance, etc.
  • Showcase a positive image of our city with the public’s pictures.
  • Help the community post useful advice.

What I would do differently

Because of the time frame I was given (approximately two days), I couldn't explore many options. I had to keep it super simple to make my deadline. With more time, I would follow other paths to make the app more appealing.
