Festival Indies

Visual identity + web design + social media + motion graphics

Client
Indies Mexico
Role
Lead designer. We were assigned to propose a redesign concept of the whole website.
Year
2019
Company
Binmatter
Project overview

Festival Indies México is an event focused on video game developers and enthusiasts from all over Mexico. It provides a space for indie creators to show off their work, provide a playable demo for the public and get more recognition, all while meeting new people with similar interests.

For this second edition, my job was to create a new visual identity to represent the improvement of the event. This time, I decided to go with brighter colors, lined iconography and colorful illustrations.

This project included a whole range of marketing designs for social media, a website, some print material and motion graphics.
