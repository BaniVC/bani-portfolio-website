ConHector

UI design + CMS + web design

ConHector is a project that seeks to help low-income communities sell and buy second-hand goods. It consists of a CMS where customers can upload products they wish to sell. Also integrated is a section to schedule and pick up deliveries.

Transactions were carried out by a third party, and contact between seller and buyer was made through a WhatsApp automatic chat, making it easier for people to keep in contact.
