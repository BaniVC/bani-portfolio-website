Innovation Desert

Visual identity + logo design + web design

Client
This was an internal project for the company I was working for at the time; a tech company focused on the development of mobile and web apps. The idea was to create a platform where people could interact.
Role
I was the sole designer on this project; I worked together with the CEO to conceptualize the idea and present it to a third party.
Year
2019
Company
Binmatter
Project overview

Innovation desert is an initiative to create networking opportunities between two border cities: Juarez, Chihuahua and El Paso, Texas. Its focus is to help small tech business contact local talent and/or each other, this to create collaborative efforts and further learning.

Something that represents both border cities is the desert climate; I used this as inspiration for the mascot and logo, as well as the colors. The website works as a search engine to find startups and companies for development services. I wanted to give it a friendlier look with a cute cactus mascot and desert elements.

Logo
